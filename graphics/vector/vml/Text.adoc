 *   .opacity(0.5)
 *   .textIndent(100);
 * stage.addChild(text);
 *
 * @param {number=} opt_x The X-coordinate (left) of the top-left corner of the text bounds.
 * @param {number=} opt_y The Y-coordinate (top) of the top-left corner of the text bounds.
